Biography of Homer Simpson

Homer Jay Simpson - one of the main characters of the animated series "The Simpsons". Homer is a sweet and funny, but sometimes rude and impolite father of the family.
He has obvious flaws: fat, bald and not very smart. He often behaves like a clown, absurd, selfish and tactless, but still remains likable.

A little more about him

Homer has three children: Bart, Lisa and Maggie. Works as a safety inspector at the Springfield Nuclear Power Plant. Homer is reluctant to work and drinks a lot.
In a few years, he became a cult figure in the United States and many other countries, including Ukraine.
In Ukraine, the animated series is broadcast on T2 digital terrestrial television.
